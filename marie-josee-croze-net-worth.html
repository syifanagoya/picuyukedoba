What is Marie-Josee Croze's Net Worth?

Marie-Josee Croze is a Canadian actress who has a net worth of $2 million. Marie-Josee Croze was born in Montreal, Quebec, Canada in February 1970. Her television debut came in the series Chambres en ville in 1989. In 1991 she starred in the TV movie Le choix. Her film debut came in 1992's La postiere.

She has starred in the films La Florida, HLA Identique, Maelstrom, Ascension, Ararat, Des chiens dans la neige, Battlefield Earth: A Saga of the year 3000, Nothing, The Barbarian Invasions, Mensonges et trahisons et plus si affinities, Ordo, Taking Lives, Munich, The Girl from the Chartreuse, Ne le dis a personne (Tell no One), La memoire des autres, Les oiseaux du ciel, La scaphandre et le papillon (The Diving Bell and the Butterfly), Jacquou le Croquant, Deux jours a tuer, Le noveau protocole, Vivre!, Je l'aimais, Un balcon sur la mer, La Cuisine, Liberte, Murder on the Orient Express, Meres et Filles, Another Silence, La Certosa di Parma, Collision, and Calvary. Croze won Best Actress at the 2003 Cannes Film Festival. She won Genie Awards in 2001 and 2004 and Jutra Awards in the same years. Marie-Josee obtained French citizenship in 2012.
