Dave Navarro News - Us Weekly

Name:Dave Navarro

Hometown:Santa Monica, CA

Relationship:Single

Birthday:June 7, 1967

About

Dave Navarro revved up the music scene with his stellar guitar skills and edgy personality as a member of the rock groups The Red Hot Chili Peppers and Jane’s Addiction. The rocker’s high-profile marriage to Baywatch babe Carmen Electra was nothing if not a tumultuous one, and the couple—in true rockstar fashion—invited MTV cameras to document their 2004 wedding preparations for the reality series ‘Til Death Do Us Part.

In 2006, the high-profile pair announced their separation. After early success in Jane’s Addiction, Navarro and his bandmates called it quits in 1991. Later, Navarro joined The Red Hot Chili Peppers, but left the group after being unsatisfied with the band’s product. In 2001, the rocker released a solo album, Trust No One, and teamed with The Panic Channel 2006 to offer One.
