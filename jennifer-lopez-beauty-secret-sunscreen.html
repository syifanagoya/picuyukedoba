Jennifer Lopez's skincare secret is so simple - and you're probably skipping it

This big anti-aging tip keeps her skin looking youthful.

    Jennifer Lopez is always asked about the secret to her skin. “Wherever I go, the number one question people ask is, ‘What do you do for your skin?’” the star told Elle in her new February cover interview.  And for good reason. JLo’s skin is always glowing, and there’s not a wrinkle in sight.  

    MORE: Jennifer Lopez looks ethereal with platinum blonde hair transformation

    So, what does she do to make sure her skin stays in tip-top shape? The crooner said there are “Five S’s” on her skincare regimen - and one big one is sunscreen. It’s something everyone should use to protect their skin daily from the sun’s effects, which can speed up the process of aging when sunscreen isn’t worn.

    As for JLo’s remaining Four S’s, that keep her looking “youthful and timeless at every age”, those are “sleep, serums, supplements, and “vivir sano,” which is Spanish for “living a healthy lifestyle.” The mom of two describes her skincare regimen as holistic and included those sunscreen, serum, and supplement elements in her JLO Beauty collection, which launched at the beginning of the year. 

    All of the products contain another one of her secret skincare ingredients - olive oil - which she has used since she was a little girl. 

    JLo shows off her glowing skin

    JLo revealed her children are already trying to steal her beauty products, with Sam and Emme both trying on face masks she’s had in the past.  “I’ve tried everything from the cheap products at the drugstore to the most expensive stuff—and some of these masks can run you $50 to $200, okay? They are expensive,” she told the mag. “My kids are, like, trying to put it on, and I’m like, ‘Don’t touch that mask, that’s too expensive to play around with.’”
