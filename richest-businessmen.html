What is Ken Cage's Net Worth?

Ken Cage is an American reality television personality who has a net worth of $10 million. Ken Cage is best known for being featured on the Discovery Channel reality television series Airplane Repo as a repossession agent who recovers aircraft from owners that have not been able to keep on their payments to financial institutions. The show debuted in 2011 but took a two year hiatus and came back in 2013. Season two of Airplane Repo finished in October 2014. Cage has worked for more than 20 years in the banking and collections industry and has worked for DaimlerChrysler Financial Services and JP Morgan.

He has done more than 1,200 repos totaling over $300 million. He joined the International Recovery & Remarketing Group or IRG Group in 2005. He has done repossessions in all 50 states as well as in several foreign countries. He has been a member of the National Aircraft Finance Association, American Society of Industrial Security, Aircraft Owners & Pilots Association, International Society of Healthcare Safety and Security, International Association of Marine Investigators, and National Association of Chiefs of Police.
