What is Meadow Sisto's Net Worth?

Meadow Sisto is an American actress who has a net worth of $2 million. Meadow Sisto was born in Grass Valley, California in September 1972. One of her best known roles came in her film debut starring as Caroline Harvey in the movie Captain Ron in 1992. Sisto also appeared in the movies The Last Time I Committed Suicide in 1997, Crossing Fields in 1997, Can't Hardly Wait in 1998, Beach Movie in 1998, Don Plum in 2001, Ted Bundy in 2002, and In Memory of My Father in 2005. Meadow Sisto appeared in episodes of the TV series Tales of the City, Sweet Temptation, Twisted Desire, Sliders, The Sentinel, NYPD Blue, Nash Bridges, L.A.

Doctors, and The Tick. She also appeared in the shorts Three Women of Pain and Men Named Milo, Women Named Greta. Sisto was nominated for a Young Artist Award in 1993 for Best Young Actress Starring in a Motion Picture for Captain Ron. Meadow's brother is actor Jeremy Sisto.
