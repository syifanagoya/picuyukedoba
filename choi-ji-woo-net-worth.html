What is Choi Ji-woo's Net Worth?

Choi Ji-woo is a South Korean actress who has a net worth of $15 million. Choi Ji-woo was born Choi Mi-hyang in Paju, Gyeonggi Province, South Korea in June 1975. She was discovered after winning a talent audition in 1994. She appeared in an episode of Best Theater in 1995 and then changed her name. In 2002 she starred as Yoo-jin Jung in the TV series Winter Sonata.

Ji-woo has starred in the movies Park Bong-gon gachulageon, Hallelujah, The Hole, Taekwondo, First Kiss, Nowhere to Hide, Moumantai 2, The Romantic President, Everybody Has a Little Secret, Shadowless Sword, and Yeolliji. She has appeared on many television dramas such as Beautiful Days and Stairway to Heaven as well as music videos and variety shows. Choi won Best new Actress awards at the Baeksang Arts Awards and Golden Cinematography Awards in 1998. She has won awards from the Golden Disk Awards, MBC Drama Awards, SBS Drama Awards, KBS Drama Awards, Andre Kim Best Star Awards, Proud Korean Awards, The Motion Picture Association of Korea, Dior Timeless Beauty Awards, International Cultural Industry Exchange Foundation Awards, World Tourism Cities Forum, Asia Model Festival Awards, and Seoul International Social Work Conference.
